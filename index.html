What is it about?

K'uychi Project is all about integrating traditional culture with technology, and the latest product is a simple mobile game featuring K'uychi, the book's heroine. This game integrates a character from peruvian mythology, Kon, god of rain and wind who became evil after humans forgot to bring him offerings. He punished them by turning the fertile lands in arid deserts, reason why the peruvian coast is mostly desertic. In the game K'uychi moves from side to side trying to collect rain dropplets while avoiding falling Kon, who is represented as having a feline face and a boneless body. My inspiration for Kon's illustration comes from Paracas textiles. During the game K'uychi has a verbal reponse whenever she collects an item, unu, para, haylli with rain droplets, sonqo with hearts, k'uychi with rainbow droplets and hearts. She also expresses pain when Kon falls on her.
